Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequenc, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, intoduction, or brife scene preceding the main action or plot of a film; contract to epilogue
